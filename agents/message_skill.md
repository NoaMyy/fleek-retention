# Fleek Retention — Message Drafting Rules

## Guiding Principles

- SMS-length (≤160 chars per message). Clear, no fluff.
- Always address the account by first name if available.
- Never mention a competitor. Never promise what you can't deliver.
- Use British English spelling.

---

## Funnel Stage Framework by Segment

### AT_RISK (HEALTHY_AM declining)
Framework: **Challenger Sale**
- Lead with a surprising insight or data point about their spend drop.
- Challenge their assumption that the relationship is fine.
- Offer a specific, concrete save action (call, custom batch).
- Touch 1 — Reframe: "I noticed X changed. Here's what I'm seeing."
- Touch 2 — Social proof: "Other accounts in your tier reversed this by…"
- Touch 3 — Scarcity: "I can hold a slot this week. After that stock moves."

### BROKER_RELIANT
Framework: **Cialdini Scarcity + Reciprocity**
- Acknowledge they rely on you (it's not a criticism).
- Show them what self-serve unlocks that brokering can't (speed, more SKUs).
- Touch 1 — Awareness: "Did you know you can do X yourself in the app?"
- Touch 2 — Social proof: "Accounts like yours that moved to self-serve grew X% in 90 days."
- Touch 3 — Scarcity: "I'm freeing up my sourcing time this week — last chance to get set up before I'm full."

Rung adjustments:
- not_started: explain what self-serve is, zero jargon.
- stalled: acknowledge they tried, remove the blocker ("What got in the way?").
- moving: celebrate progress, name the next step.
- nearly_graduated: one more nudge. Offer a walkthrough or live session.

### SELF_SERVE_HEADROOM / SELF_SERVE_MATURE
Framework: **PLG social proof**
- Reference their existing behaviour (app days, recent PDP views) as proof they're engaged.
- Show them one specific feature they haven't tried yet.
- Keep it product-led — no AM dependency.
- Touch 1 — Awareness: what the feature does in one line.
- Touch 2 — Social proof: how other buyers use it.
- Touch 3 — Scarcity / urgency: limited availability or upcoming stock drop.

Feature nudge angles:
- video_call: "Jump on a 15-min video call — our buyer team will hand-pick a batch just for you."
- chat: "Message us in-app. Get a reply faster than email, matched to what you've been browsing."
- bundle: "Build a bundle — mix categories, set your price, ship in one go."

## Message Length & Tone

| Touch | Tone        | Length  |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1     | Warm, brief | 80–120 chars |
| 2     | Confident   | 100–140 chars |
| 3     | Direct      | 60–100 chars |

## Output Format

Return exactly two variants — A and B — for every account:
- Variant A: lead with the feature/insight.
- Variant B: lead with the outcome/social proof.
Both variants must be complete, standalone messages.

Do NOT include headers, JSON keys, or labels in the message body itself.
